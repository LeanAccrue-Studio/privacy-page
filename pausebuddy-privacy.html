PauseBuddy - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-08

PauseBuddy (“the App”) is a free, lightweight productivity and wellbeing tool designed to help you manage screen time. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how data is handled.

1. Information We Collect

PauseBuddy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

App Usage Data (Usage Access Permission)

  • We access app usage time and app package names.
  • This is used only to measure how long you use selected apps and notify you when limits are reached.
  • All data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

2. How We Use Information

All information accessed is used solely to:

  • Display app usage time
  • Reset daily timers
  • Send reminders when time limits are exceeded

3. No Data Sharing

PauseBuddy does not share data with third parties, use advertising networks, or send analytics data. The App operates fully offline.

4. Children’s Privacy

PauseBuddy is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Security

Since PauseBuddy does not transmit personal information externally, your data remains entirely on your device.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
