Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our app (the “App”) handles information and data when you use it.

Overview

The App is designed with privacy in mind. It operates primarily offline and does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to our servers.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information.

  • No account registration is required
  • No names, email addresses, or phone numbers are collected
  • No analytics or tracking identifiers are used

Images and Text Processing

All image scanning, OCR (text recognition), and translation features are performed locally on your device.

  • Photos you take or select are processed only on your device
  • Extracted text remains on your device
  • No images or text are uploaded to external servers

Internet Access

The App may optionally use internet access only when you explicitly enable online features (such as downloading language models or updates).

We do not transmit your personal content (images or text) during these operations.

Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties.

  • No advertising partners
  • No analytics services
  • No data brokers

Data Storage

Any data generated by the App (such as scanned text or saved images) is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by you at any time.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected, the App is safe to use for all ages.

Your Control

You are always in control of your data:

  • You can delete app data by clearing storage or uninstalling the App
  • You choose which images or documents to process

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
